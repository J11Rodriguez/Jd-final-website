About JD Landon

My name is JD Landon and I am the owner and founder of jdsafespace. I have been in law enforcement since April of 1993 when I started with the Springfield Missouri Police Department. I have held both municipal and county commissions during my law enforcement career. My municipal commissions were through the Springfield Missouri Police Department and the Willard Missouri Police Department. My county commission was through the Greene County Missouri Sheriff’s office. I have been involved in martial arts since 1982 and have studied several different styles through the years which include Tae Kwon-Do, Hapkido, Tai Chi, Muay Thai, Shoot boxing and Kickboxing. I have also spent several years studying several different weapons. I hold a second-degree black belt in the World Tae Kwon-Do Federation (WTF) and the United States Tae Kwon-Do Federation (USTF) and have been an instructor since 1984. I graduated Ozarks Technical Community College with an AA degree in 2006. I then graduated Evangel University with a Bachelor’s of Science degree in Human Services (counseling and case management). I went on to graduated Missouri State University with a Master of Science Administrative Studies (Business & Criminal Justice) degree in 2009. I became a college professor in 2012 teaching criminal Justice classes. I want to utilize my education, training, skills, and life experience’s teaching others on identifying threats, create plans of action and how to implement those action plans. I also want to show how implementing those new ways of conduct will cultivate a safer environment making for a better and more enjoyable life for themselves. I also want to show business and corporations how to create a safer more productive environment for their employees. Employers who are proactive in cultivating a safer environment could lower the liability, minimize an incident or even prevent an incident from occurring.
